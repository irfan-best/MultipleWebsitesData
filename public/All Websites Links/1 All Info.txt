Mode specific keys:
        Escape                          when we are in searchMode enter shift to exit
        
        1 to 0                          when we are in multipleElementsSelectionMode, then 1 will click on 
                                        first img of current row, 2 will click 2nd img of current row..
        
        l                               when we are in multipleElementsSelectionMode, only selected img names,
                                        will get copied

        m                               when we are in multipleElementsSelectionMode, move button will get clicked
        shift                           when we are in multipleElementsSelectionMode, submit btn will be clicked
        number < no_Of_Imgs_Per_Row     when we are in multipleElementsSelectionMode, then on current, if 1 key 
                                        is entered then first element of row is clicked, if 2 key entered then
                                        2nd element of current row is clicked and so on...

        M                               when we are in M_Mode, will exit the M_Mode

        ;                               when we are in fileNameChangeMode, will include img extension
        Escape                          when we are in fileNameChangeMode, will exit fileNameChangeMode
